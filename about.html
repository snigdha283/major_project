The Novel Coronavirus commonly known as COVID-19, is a disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 virus. It causes serious long-term complications such as cardiovascular and respiratory abnormalities. From first being identified in China, late 2019 it is undergoing several mutations and is posing a major public health and governance challenges. Various clinical studies and researchers across the globe have established that Chest CT Scans are providing accurate clinical diagnosis on detection of COVID-19 As the traditional gold standard RT-PCR Testing methodology might give more false positive and false negative results than the desired rates. AI has proven to be the driving force in developing various COVID-19 management tools. The goal of the project is to detect COVID-19 on chest CT Scan images. Through this project we will be able to provide a model which will analyse the CT Scan images and predict whether the person is infected with COVID-19 or not
